About i.Fest

i.Fest is the largest technical fest of Gujarat with its versatility in technical, cultural, and social events, with its intellectual and social-welfare oriented community and its extremely enthusiastic participants. With over 3500 participants attending the fest every year, i.Fest provides the student community with a humongous platform to interact with like-thinkers and inculcate innovative and competitive skills within young minds. Such festive technical events are held with an aim to grab the best out of a bundle of ideas constantly playing around within a group of people so that they are given a voice as well as an opportunity to ask for monetary as well as technical help from a capable institute or a company wherever needed.

A Cryptic Cosmos

Mystery and universe are seemingly two inseparable words. We often synonymize the universe as an unexplored space. We, the homo sapiens, are currently in a space of disconnectedness and isolation and yet so wholesome within ourselves, unfolding the mysteries of our being along with the universe. The theme revolves around the idea of exploring our inner selves and unwrapping the parts of us which we could not ever since. During the pandemic, when most of us were locked indoors, we gained an incredibly cherishable opportunity to introspect ourselves, isolating our brains from the busy routines of “life”.

i.Fest ‘21 - Cryptic Cosmos, will stand in celebration of the secret treasures the pandemic brought us to, power to move forward with the acceptance of disheartening mishaps, the unfolding of mysteries of our very own little universe and for the progress of humankind in helping us get through it.
